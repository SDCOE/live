#innovateSD LIVE!

Exclusive online virtual summit hosted by the San Diego County Office of Education Innovation Division to bring together educators and industry partners from around the world to explore how we can scale, support, and develop innovative educational programs. 

Live streaming event on November 1, 2018!

REGISTER

Overview

Discover the latest in IT, EdTech, Career Technical Education (CTE), Virtual & Augmented Reality in live streaming webinars with industry experts, educators, school and district leaders from San Diego County and around the world! Hear the very latest from leaders and practioners and dive deep into innovative approaches, technologies, and solutions across various strands.

This free online virtual summit on November 1, 2018 is designed to inspire and educate you about new programs and tools and help you develop the skills to launch, deploy or implement in your class, school, or organization.

30+ Sessions

Join practictioners and industry experts as they cover a broad range of topics across core themes: Emerging Tech, CTE, EdTech, IT, & Career Advancement

Live Q&A

Get your educational and technical questions answered by colleagues, peers, and industry experts.

On-Demand

Can't attend the live stream? Access on-demand videos of archived sessions to expand your knowledge.

Global

Connect and engage with peers around the world and get ahead of educational trends that impact in your classroom, school, or organization.

Why Attend?

We recognize practictioners and organizations are in many stages of their innovation journey, from exploration to power-users and everything in between. This free online virtual conference will enable you to maximize innovation with access to peers, industry partners, and new tools and platforms.

Who Should Attend?

Whether you are new to education or an experienced educator, you can learn something new at #InnovateSD Live. #InnovateSD Live is designed to help you learn more about new programs, tools, or applications to implement in your respective organization.

Partners

Interested in becoming an #InnovateSD LIVE partner? Contact james@classroomofthefuture.org to discuss opportunities to be showcased throughout our online virtual summit.
